Stress

Various situations or life events can cause stress. When we encounter new experiences or when a situation is beyond our control, we may feel more stressed than usual. Stress cannot be eliminated, but we cope with stress in different ways. If you're having difficulty managing stress, seek professional help to find ways to better manage it.

Depression

Depression is a deep and enduring feeling of sadness or despair. Initially, you may find yourself lacking enthusiasm. You continue to go about your daily life, but everything feels heavier and futile. Over time, you may engage in self-harming behaviors. If you consistently lose interest in things you used to enjoy, seek expert help.

learn more

Traumatized

To recover from trauma, you need to recognize it first. Traumatic events can cause lasting pain. Sometimes, childhood trauma only affects you as an adult. You may feel emotionally numb or easily triggered, and often, you recall past events that hurt you. Seek a mental health expert assessment if you feel you have experienced trauma.

Personality Disorder

Is your behavior inconsistent and confusing to those around you? Does your behavioral pattern affect school, work, self-control, and your relationships? Do you struggle with socializing and/or controlling impulses? Is it difficult for you to regulate your thoughts and emotions?If any of these questions resonate with you, seek an assessment from a mental health professional.
